FAQ

How to use Soundit?

To transfer your music between streaming services you just need to follow 4 simple steps:
1) Create an account and connect with streaming services.
2) Select playlists you want to transfer.
3) Edit title/description, select which songs you want to transfer.
4) Click 'convert' and enjoy your music!

What streaming services does Soundit support?

You can see the complete list of streaming services that Soundit supports on our home page. For now, we support Spotify and YouTube. But don't worry! We're planning on adding some more in the future.

Is Soundit free?

Yes! Soundit is 100% free to use for everyone. You can transfer or delete your playlists anytime you want!

How do I remove Soundit access to Spotify or YouTube service?

To remove Soundit access to other services, please go to the settings or your profile page. There you will see two links to disconnect with music service.

Does Soundit remove your playlists from the source platform?

No! We are just copying your music to the new platform, We do not change or delete anything on the source platform.

When the transfer is finished, will it show a list of songs that did not transfer?

Yes, the songs that failed to transfer will be listed at the end of the process.
